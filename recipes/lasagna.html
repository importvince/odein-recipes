How to make delicious Lasagna

Lasagna Ingredients

  • 1 lb italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup choped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 28oz of crushed tomatoes
  • 13oz of tomato sauce
  • 12oz of tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 Tbsp white sugar
  • 4 Tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 & 1/2 tsp basil leaves
  • 1 & 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1/1 tsp fennel
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16oz ricotta
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella
  • 3/4 cup parmesan cheese
